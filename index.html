ABOUT US

10Fifteen Events is a full-service wedding and event planning company dedicated to turning special occasions into extraordinary celebrations.

Whether you’re planning a romantic wedding, a lively party, a heartfelt communion, or any milestone event, we take care of every detail so you can enjoy the moment to its fullest.

Born from a passion for celebration and inspired by the very date that sparked our name, 10Fifteen Events blends professional planning expertise with personal care and creativity.

We work closely with each client to understand your vision and bring it to life with style, precision, and a touch of fun.

From initial concept and design to seamless coordination on the day itself, our services are tailored to fit your needs — whether that’s an intimate communion party, an elegant wedding, or a vibrant birthday celebration.

With meticulous organization and a commitment to excellence, we ensure your event is truly one-of-a-kind and memorable for everyone who attends.

Let’s plan something unforgettable together — a day that reflects your personality, your joy, and your unique story.
